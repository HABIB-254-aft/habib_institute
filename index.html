Meet the Founder

Abdirahman Mohamed

Who We Are

Habib Institute provides accessible, practical, and flexible education for youth, job-seekers, and professionals across Kenya, Somalia, and the Somali diaspora. Our mission is to empower learners with the skills needed to succeed academically, socially, and economically.
