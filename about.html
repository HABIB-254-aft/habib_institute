About Habib Institute

Empowering learners across East Africa with languages, digital skills, and career development.

Meet the Founder

Abdirahman Mohamed

Abdirahman Mohamed founded Habib Institute to create a bridge between traditional education and modern skill-building. His passion for community development, languages, and technology inspired the birth of an institution that empowers learners through practical knowledge and accessible training.

Who We Are

Habib Institute of Languages & Technology is an innovative learning institution dedicated to providing accessible, affordable, and practical education for youth, job-seekers, and professionals across Kenya, Somalia, and the Somali diaspora. We offer programs that strengthen communication skills, digital literacy, and career readiness — empowering learners to succeed in school, work, and life.

Our Mission

To equip learners with language proficiency, digital skills, and professional knowledge that open doors to education, employment, and global opportunities.

Our Vision

To become East Africa’s leading training institution where students gain modern skills, confidence, and the ability to succeed in an ever-changing world.
